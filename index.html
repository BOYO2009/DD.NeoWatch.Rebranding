Who are we?

We are Neowatch, the best smartwatch company for third world buyers. We sell to people who don't have the money to buy the expensive $500 Apple watch. As Neowatch, we've always focused on practical features that matter most to our users. Our watches offer always-on, easily readable displays and exceptional battery life, often lasting up to a week on a single charge, far surpassing most competitors. Neowatch devices are cross-platform, working with both iOS and Android, and feature reliable, customizable notifications and a vast library of apps and watch faces. The use of physical buttons makes navigation simple, even with gloves or wet hands, while our water-resistant, durable design ensures our watches can handle daily life. Neowatch's affordability and strong community support, even after our acquisition and the end of official support, further cemented our reputation. Altogether, Neowatch's blend of simplicity, reliability, and user empowerment made us a beloved and influential pioneer in the smartwatch industry.
